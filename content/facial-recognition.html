Different age stages

Upload photos of yourself showing you at different stages of your life. This will help you determine whether the facial recognition system is able to identify you at different ages. Observe how the percentage changes and whether the system recognises you in different stages of life.

Similar faces in comparison

Use a picture in which several people look similar. The facial recognition system can easily be confused with similar faces. Test whether it can distinguish between similar faces. Pay attention to how the percentage reacts and whether the system can correctly identify your face.

Multiple times, the same face

Use several photos of yourself in which your face appears multiple times. If the system is confronted with several identical faces, uncertainties may arise. Investigate which of your faces are better recognised and how you can influence this process. Note the changes in the percentage and the green markings.
