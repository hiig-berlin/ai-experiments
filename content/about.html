About the project

On this website, we would like to give you an insightful look into the world of artificial intelligence on the subject of facial recognition. For this purpose we have designed two experiments on facial detection and facial recognition as part of the research project Artificial intelligence, explained in human terms research project.

Facial detection

Find out whether your face is recognised as a face by the facial detection system using the camera on your laptop or on your mobile phone. In the experiment you can then try out how you can outwit the face detection system. Test how it reacts to different tricks, for example by covering your face or changing your perspective. Try out in which cases the system recognises a face better or worse.

Facial recognition

For this experiment, we're going one step further with you. Explore whether the AI in this experiment accurately recognises your face in photos and which circumstances make this recognition better or worse. To do this, you can change the lighting conditions or use a slightly different perspective. Upload several photos showing you in different stages of your life, or use images of other people who may resemble you. Can you trick the facial recognition system?
