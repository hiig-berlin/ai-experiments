Facial detection

Here you have the opportunity to test the facial detection system and see how it reacts to different tricks. The system recognises a face when it is highlighted in green. The percentage displayed indicates the probability of the system detecting a face.

Wir legen großen Wert auf Datenschutz: Deine persönlichen Daten werden nicht gespeichert, und die Systeme arbeiten lokal. Das heißt, sie werden auf deinem Gerät ausgeführt und beim Schließen des Browsers gelöscht.

Cover your face

Test the limits of the system by covering part of your face. Use your hand, a piece of paper or anything you have at hand. Pay special attention to how the detection reacts to covered eyes. Observe how the percentage in the circle changes, when you cover one or both eyes.

Change the perspective

The AI is trained to recognise faces from the front. Get creative - tilt or turn your head to the side. Pay attention to how the colours and percentages of the facial detection change. Can you tell the difference?

Change your surroundings

Test how the system reacts to changes on your face. Paint your face with unusual colours or add temporary markings. What effect do they have on the detection and the display in the circle?

Paint your face

The environment can influence the work of the AI. Play with the light: Darken the room or illuminate it differently by switching the light off or on. Hold the camera in such a way that the sun shines into the picture. How do they affect the facial detection?

Enjoy experimenting and discovering! You are helping to better understand facial detection and explore the possibilities as well as limits of this technology.
